Kathryn Hurchla

Principal

Kathryn is a data developer and designer. She founded Data Design Dimension in 2020 to build visual data solutions across a variety of business and social domains. She earned a master's degree in data analytics and visualization after learning the art of getting things done with technology in two world-renowned organizations. She writes and speaks about data science in the real world and contributes to uplifting data communities as a Plotly Dash Ambassador and an author liaison with Data Visualization Society's Nightingale Editorial Committee.


She chases joy by mountain biking or just practicing the Japanese art of forest bathing on local trails in Philadelphia or in the Northeast Kingdom of Vermont. A wanderlust to explore the world and communication in all its forms has led her to travel where people predominantly speak Spanish, Mandarin and Cantonese Chinese, and Central Asian languages. At home she enjoys the diversity of Philly where 14% of her neighbors were born outside the United States and 24% speak a language other than English (higher than the national average in 2019 according to Data USA).
